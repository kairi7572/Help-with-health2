Help with health

Fiber’s Impact on Health Fiber can help with health conditions like allergies, heavy metal toxicity, cognitive function, asthma, skin rashes, inflammatory bowel diseases, osteoporosis, and more. Because those fruits and veggies are right in our grocery store, though, we often overlook them or take them for granted as medicine. For the sake of your skin, gut, and lifespan, you really should give fiber a shot.

In this article, I’ll explain what fiber is, the many forms it comes in, and how to use it effectively and strategically for particular health benefits. Let’s dive in. If there were a drug that did all of the things that fiber does, I’m convinced it would be a million dollars a dose. If you account for the fact that fiber also has very few side effects, we’re looking at an incredibly effective, safe, and valuable health intervention that far outpaces any pill we’ve got.
